About Us

Elements Resort Homestay is situated in Mutharikalam, Neelom, Korom. Among the facilities of this property, a Restaurant, 24 hours front desk and room service, along with free wifi. The Property provides a natural pond built in a classic traditional kerala style, a garden and porshe rooms with garden views from the nicely built balconies.

All rooms in the Elements Resort are built in a porshe style with lots of wood work and safety deposit cupboards. There are 2 well equipped rooms in every villa. At with bed linen and towels.

Traditional Nadan Breakfast options are available each morning and Kerala style lunch N dinner too. If it is very particular continental thandhoor items also will be provided at Elements Resort.
